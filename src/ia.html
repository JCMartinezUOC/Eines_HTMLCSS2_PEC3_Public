Contestants

Our first contestant, Maria, has prepared a delightful dish of seafood paella. Her recipe includes fresh shrimp, mussels, and clams, cooked to perfection in a savory saffron-infused broth. She garnishes the dish with parsley and lemon, creating a visually stunning and mouthwatering presentation.

The second contestant, Juan, presents his take on traditional Mexican mole. This rich, complex sauce is made from a blend of chili peppers, spices, chocolate, and nuts, served over tender pieces of chicken. Juan's mole recipe has been passed down through generations, and his unique twist includes adding a hint of cinnamon for extra warmth and depth of flavor.
